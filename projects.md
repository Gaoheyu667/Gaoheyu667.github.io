# Research Projects
## UMLS-KG Driven MM-Agent Medical Assistance AI Platform
**Project Leader | Provincial Key Research Project**  
*Oct. 2025 – Jun. 2026*

**Project Background**: Built a medical auxiliary decision-making platform based on multi-agent collaborative consensus algorithm, to address the shortage of grassroots general practitioners, weak interdisciplinary reasoning and the black-box problem of large models.

**My Work**:
- Independently completed full-stack development and algorithm experiments, built a LangChain-based multi-agent scheduling framework
- Constructed a knowledge graph retrieval module based on the UMLS medical knowledge base
- Built 5,000 medical sentiment annotated corpus and completed LLM fine-tuning with LoRA
- Designed a dynamic weighted adversarial debate consensus algorithm for multi-agent reasoning
- Developed PC-side interactive interface with Flask + Vue, and completed cloud deployment

**Key Achievements**:
- Zero-shot reasoning accuracy improved from 65.3% to 79.1%
- Single-case inference time reduced from 18.5s to 9.2s
- Sentiment recognition accuracy improved from 78% to 89%
- Published 1 first-author academic paper based on this project

## Ascend Intelligent Care: ICU Full-Domain Monitoring Intelligent Vehicle
**Core Member | National-level Research Project**  
*Nov. 2025 – Jun. 2026*

**Project Background**: Built a multimodal severe risk prediction system based on CNN-Transformer fusion and graph attention network, to solve discrete multimodal physiological time-series data, insufficient single-modal prediction accuracy and difficulty in early severe warning in ICU.

**My Work**:
- Built the LSTM time-series image processing and feature prediction branch under the multimodal fusion framework
- Completed data cleaning and encoding of ICU image datasets and continuous physiological sign time-series data
- Developed the full code pipeline for LSTM model training
- Conducted multiple ablation control experiments to compare model performance with and without LSTM time-series branch

**Key Achievements**:
- Realized joint modeling of medical images and vital sign time-series data
- Implemented visualized interaction of disease risk grading combined with Huawei Ascend development board
